Pan Galactic gargle blaster

The best drinks in existence, from "hitchinker's Guide to the Galaxy"

An intricate and dangerous cctail, invented by Zaphod Beeblebrox.

Purpoted to make you feel like "having your brains smashed out by a slice of lemon round a large gold brick"

Ingredients

  • Old Janx Spirit (1 Bottle)
  • Sea water from Santraiginus V (1 Measure)
  • Acturan Mega-gin cubes(3 cubes)
  • Fallian marsh gas (4 liters)
  • Qualactin Hypermint extract (1 teaspoon)
  • Angolian Suntiger tooth
  • Zamphuor
  • Olive(1 oliv)

Recipe instructions ( taken from the book)

  1. Take the juice from one ottle of O1 ' Janx Spirit.
  2. Pour into it measure of water from the seas of Santraiginus V - Oh, that Santraginean seawater! Oh those Santraginean fish!
  3. Allow three cubes of Artucan Mega-gin to melt into the mixture(it must be properly iced or the benzene is lost).
  4. Allow four litres of Fallian marsh gas to bubble through it, in memory of all those happy hikers who have died of dark Qualactin Zones, subtle, sweet and mystic.
  5. Over the back of a silver spoon float a measure of Qualactin Hypermint extract, redolent of all the heady odours of the dark Qualactin Zones, subtitle, sweet and mystic.
  6. Drop in the tooth of an Algolian Suntiger. Watch it dissolve, spreading the fires of the Algolian Suns deep into the heart of the drink.
  7. Sprinkle Zamphuor
  8. Add an olive
  9. Drink... but.. very carefully...
