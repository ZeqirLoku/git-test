Charlie's Milk Steaker

A scrumptios steak dinner, best served with free-range jelly beans

The preferred meal of Charlie Kelly, from "It's always Sunny in Philidelphia"

Only thefinest cuts of beef should be used, and is delicious when served immediately, or after farmenting in the trash for a week

Ingredients

  • Beef cut of choice (Wagyu or Kobe is ideal)
  • Cow milk, fresh and unpasteriuzed
  • (optional) Free range, cruelty-free jelly beans

Recipe Insructions

  1. Bring fresh milk to a roaring boil
  2. Tear apart beff into palatable chunks with your hands
  3. Immediately pour beef chunks into boiling milk
  4. Let beef boil for at least 18 hours, until boiled over the hands
  5. Remove beef from milk, and serve on your finest china with a side of raw jelly beans
